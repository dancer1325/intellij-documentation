## Zoom IDE

  1. Go to View | Appearance and select Zoom IDE.

  2. To preview different zoom levels, hover over the options in the popup.

![IDE Zoom level](https://resources.jetbrains.com/help/img/idea/2025.3/zoom_level_popup.png)
  3. Click the preferred zoom level to apply the changes.




  1. Press `Ctrl+Alt+S` to open settings and then select Appearance & Behavior | Appearance.

  2. In the Accessibility section, click the Zoom field and enter your value.

![Enter a custom zoom level](https://resources.jetbrains.com/help/img/idea/2025.3/enter-zoom-level-preview.png)
  3. Click OK to apply the changes.
